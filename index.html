Air Freight

KCC provides integrated One-Stop Logistics Service including international transportation, customs clearance, warehousing, and domestic trucking via KCC branch offices and worldwide partner network. With KCC’s state-of-the-art Logistics Information Systems, customers have full visibility of their cargo status from origin to destination. KCC is a member of IATA (#01-19440/0012) and offers complete air forwarding and consolidation services.

  • Pick up and delivery
  • Packing services
  • Customs clearance
  • Letter of Credit
  • Cargo insurance
  • Re-forwarding cargo (Sea & Air, Air & Air)
  • Door to door arrangement
  • Cargo movement monitoring via internet
Main Commodities
  • Garments, Electronic Goods, Machinery / Equipment
  • Oversize cargo
Main Carriers
  • ASIA: Korean Air, Asiana Air, China Air, UPS, Northwest
  • C & S Americas: UPS, MAS Air, Varig Air, MAS Air, Lan Chile, Aero Mexpress
  • EUROPE & OTHERS: Air France, Lufthansa, KLM, Cargolux, Alitalia

  • Pick up and delivery
  • Packing & crating
  • Letter of credit
